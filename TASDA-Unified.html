About the District Chief Executive

👩‍💼

Hon. District Chief Executive

District Chief Executive — Tatale District

Committed to transformational leadership, accountable governance, and accelerated development of the Tatale District in the Northern Region of Ghana.

Vision: A prosperous, self-reliant Tatale District where every citizen has equal access to opportunities and quality public services.

Mission: To deliver efficient, transparent, people-centred governance promoting sustainable development across every community.

Local Government

About Tatale District

📍
Location
Located in the Northern Region of Ghana, bordering Saboba and Chereponi districts.
👥
Population
Over 48,000 people across 48 communities, predominantly farming families.
🏘️
Communities
Key towns include Tatale, Sanguli, Nakpayili, and Gbungbaliga.
👑
Traditional Authorities
Governed in partnership with local chiefs and traditional councils.
🌾
Economic Activities
Agriculture is the primary livelihood — cereals, legumes, and livestock.
🏞
Tourist Attractions
Scenic savanna landscapes and cultural festivals throughout the year.

Privacy Policy

The Office of the District Chief Executive, Tatale District, is committed to protecting the privacy of citizens who use this website. Information submitted via complaint, appointment, or contact forms is used solely to respond to your request and improve district services.

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties. All forms are protected against spam and unauthorised access. This site uses HTTPS encryption and standard government security practices to safeguard your data.

For questions about this policy, contact the District Assembly office directly.
